英语演讲
I have a dream

Today I would like to talk about a topic that is very close to our heart, “dream”.

Do you still remember your first dream? Even though that everyone has different dreams, but they all have a beautiful purpose. Such as the man who spoke for the freedom of the African Americans is killed at last, but we can learn the hot heart from him. Think of what can we do for the world. With the fast developing of the modern technology, the temperature is rising and it causes to the Green House. What we can do is to save more resource, and use clean energy, to let our earth calm down.

I am always on the way to achieve my dream. I’m very interested in robot. When I try to make a new one, the rechargeable battery is my first choice for the power source. I also collect old cardboard as the body instead of using plastic board.

I will always follow my dream and encourage others to stand with me, I believe that many hands make my dream come true faster.

That’s all for my speech, thank you for listening.

中文译文/Chinese

我有一个梦想

今天我想谈谈一个非常贴近我们内心的话题，“梦想”。
你还记得你的第一个梦吗?即使每个人都有不同的梦想，但他们都有一个美丽的目的。比如那个为非裔美国人的自由说话的人最后被杀了，但我们可以从他身上学到火热的心。想想我们能为这个世界做些什么。随着现代科技的快速发展，气温不断上升，这导致了温室效应。我们能做的就是节约更多的资源，使用清洁能源，让我们的地球平静下来。
我一直在实现梦想的路上。我对机器人很感兴趣。当我想做一个新的，可充电电池是我的首选电源。我也收集旧纸板作为主体，而不是使用塑料板。
我会一直追随我的梦想，并鼓励别人和我站在一起，我相信很多人的帮助会让我的梦想更快实现。
我的演讲到此结束，谢谢大家的聆听。
